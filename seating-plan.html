Detailed Instructions & Logic

1. Preparation & Setup

  • Course Details: Fill in the Course Code, Name, Exam Name, Date, and Time. These will appear on the printed header.
  • Proctor Name: Entering a name here displays it on the screen, but it remains blank on the printed "Attendance Sheet" for a wet signature.

2. Seating Logic & Distribution

  • Balanced Distribution: If multiple classrooms are selected, students are shuffled globally and distributed proportional to room capacities.
  • Seniority Priority: Inside each room, students are sorted by ID. Smaller IDs (Senior students) are placed first.
  • "Front Packed" Mode: Seniors are placed in the front rows and aisle seats. Empty seats will remain at the back.
  • "Spread Out" Mode: Students are distributed evenly across the entire room to maximize distance between them.

3. Data Format

  • Use a .csv file with UTF-8 encoding.
  • Required Columns: ID, Name, Surname.
  • You can use the "Download Sample CSV Template" button to get a correctly formatted file.

4. Interactive Features

  • Drag & Drop: After generation, you can click and drag any student to swap their seat with another student or an empty seat.
  • Occupancy Stats: Check the blue panel at the top to see total student count vs. total capacity.

5. Printing Options

  • Print Attendance Sheet: Generates a professional list with signature lines, absent checkboxes, and a footer for Proctor Signature and Notes. Optimized for archiving.
  • Print Door Plan: Generates a cleaner, anonymous-looking plan suitable for posting on the classroom door. No signature lines or proctor details.
  • Note: All prints are optimized for Black & White to save ink and ensure crisp lines.
