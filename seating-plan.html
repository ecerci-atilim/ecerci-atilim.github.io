Detailed Instructions

📋 1. Preparation

  • Metadata: Fill in Course Code, Name, Exam details. These appear on the printed header.
  • Proctor: Name shown on screen; creates a signature line on the attendance sheet.

🏫 2. Classroom Selection

  • Select one or multiple rooms.
  • Check the capacity indicators (Normal vs Exam) next to room names.

⚙️ 3. Configuration

  • Capacity Mode: "Exam" reduces capacity (usually 1/2 or 1/3) for spacing.
  • Filling Strategy:
    • Packed: Sorts by ID (Seniors first), fills front/aisles.
    • Spread: Distributes students evenly across the room.

📄 4. Data Format

  • CSV File: Must contain ID, Name, Surname columns.
  • UTF-8: Ensure proper encoding for Turkish characters.
  • Use "Download Template" for a starting point.

🖱️ 5. Interaction & Printing

  • Drag & Drop: After generation, swap students by dragging them to another seat (occupied or empty).
  • Print Attendance: Professional list with signature/absent fields.
  • Print Door Plan: Anonymous layout for posting on doors.
  • Note: Printing is optimized for Black & White ink saving.
